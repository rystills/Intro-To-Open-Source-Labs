Article: Smart Questions - How to ask the question The Smart Way
This article does a good job of explaining in detail how the process of asking and answering questions on a programming forum should work. As a suggestion for answering questions, I would strongly recommend archiving any external links used as part of a solution. Often when searching for answers I find that solutions point to external sites, which have been modified or are no longer available, rendering the answer near useless. Additionally, Additionally, I would suggest continuing to update old answers as new information becomes available. Often solutions were once valid, but now point to obsolete or invalid code or procedures, and should be updated accordingly.
